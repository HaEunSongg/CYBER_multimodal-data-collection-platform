Participant Information & Consent Form

Study Title: Multimodal Behavioral Observation During Psychometric Testing Using IoT and AI

About this study

You are invited to participate in a research study, examining personality, emotions, and behavioral responses using questionnaires and interactive tasks.

What will you do?

1.Carefully read the information sheet, consent form, and debrief
2.Connect to the Heart Rate sensor; use mouse, keyboard, and camera for dynamic detection
3.Complete short questionnaires (HADS, BFI-2-S)
4.View emotional images and report your feelings using an interactive emotion map and Valence-Arousal Map

⏱ Duration: ~20-25 minutes

What data is collected?

  • Questionnaire responses
  • Emotional ratings
  • Reaction times
  • Mouse and keyboard interaction patterns
  • Camera-based observations
  • Physiological data (Heart Rate Frequency)

Risks

Some images may be emotionally uncomfortable. You may stop the study at any time by closing your browser.

Your rights

  • Participation is voluntary
  • You may withdraw at any time without penalty
  • Your data will be pseudonymized and securely stored
  • You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time.
  • No personally identifiable information will be published and collected data will only used for academic research purpose.
