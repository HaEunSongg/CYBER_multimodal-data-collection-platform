## Overview
Brief description of the project and what this memo documents.

## Source
- Dataset: Nencki Affective Picture System (NAPS)
- Rating scale: 1–9
- Access: https://lobi.nencki.edu.pl/research/8/
#NAPS image was used in this project,and 20 images was selected according to each of the 6 emotion categories,

## Selection Criteria

### Emotion Category Images (n = 20)
- One image per emotion category × gender-of-strongest-rating
- 6 emotion categories
- No image used more than once
- Selection rule: highest female-rated image → assigned to "Women" set;
  highest male-rated image → assigned to "Men" set

### Neutral Baseline / Control Images (n = 4)
- All 6 emotion ratings < 2.5
- Purpose: resting baseline / control condition

### Gender-Divergent Images (n = 4)
- Moderate-low overall emotion
- Large male vs. female rating divergence
- Purpose: scientifically interesting edge cases
